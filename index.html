📌 About Cantor Reelz

Cantor Reelz, also known as Inspector Mugarbe of the 29 Battalion, is a multifaceted Nigerian comedian, content creator, social media personality, and tech professional. His real name is Joseph Mallo Mambayat, and he hails from Plateau State, Nigeria.

Early Life and Education

Cantor Reelz finished his National Diploma in Mechanical Engineering Technology at Federal Polytechnic Nasarawa and is currently pursuing his degree at Federal University of Technology Minna (FUTMINNA). His academic background has equipped him with a unique perspective, which he incorporates into his content.

Tech Expertise

As a tech professional specializing in cybersecurity, Cantor Reelz brings innovation to his online presence, sharing insights and tips on cybersecurity awareness. He collaborates with tech companies and startups, empowering young Nigerians with tech skills.
