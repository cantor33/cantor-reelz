📌 About Cantor Reelz

Cantor Reelz, also known as Inspector Mugarbe of the 29 Battalion, is a multifaceted Nigerian comedian, content creator, social media personality, and tech professional. His real name is Joseph Mallo Mambayat, and he hails from Plateau State, Nigeria.

🎓 Education & Background

Cantor Reelz finished his National Diploma in Mechanical Engineering Technology at Federal Polytechnic Nasarawa and is currently pursuing his degree at Federal University of Technology Minna (FUTMINNA).

💻 Tech Expertise

Specializing in cybersecurity, Cantor Reelz shares tech awareness tips and collaborates with startups to empower young Nigerians with digital skills.

😂 Content Style

His digital presence as Inspector Mugarbe of the 29 Battalion focuses on campus news, social highlights, and satire. His signature catchphrase is:

"E sharp like that, I'm Inspector Mugarbe of 29 Battalion, I'm the great Gburu Gburu."

🌍 Future Plans

Cantor Reelz plans international collaborations, mentorship programs for young creators, and expansion into new entertainment formats.
